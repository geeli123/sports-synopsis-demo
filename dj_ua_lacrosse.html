Game Summary: 2021 Dublin Jerome Lacrosse State Championship vs. Upper Arlington
Winning Team: Dublin Jerome Celtics
Losing Team: Upper Arlington Golden Bears
Final Score: Dublin Jerome Celtics: 12, Upper Arlington Golden Bears: 11
Game Summary: The Dublin Jerome Celtics started strong, controlling possession and taking a 6-3 lead at halftime over the Upper Arlington Golden Bears, who struggled with turnovers and clears. Upper Arlington mounted a furious comeback in the second half, fueled by Sam Burns' five goals, eventually tying the game at 10-10 and briefly taking an 11-10 lead. However, critical turnovers by Upper Arlington on clear attempts allowed Jerome to stay in the game and tie it again. With 28 seconds remaining, Carter Barco scored the game-winning goal for Jerome, and the Celtics held on during a final mad scramble for possession to secure the state championship.
