Winning Team: Ohio State
Losing Team: Notre Dame
Final Score: Ohio State: 21, Notre Dame: 10
Game Summary: The game started with Notre Dame taking an early lead, with their defense effectively containing Ohio State's prolific offense and holding a 10-7 lead at halftime. In the second half, Ohio State adjusted their strategy, relying more on a physical running game and short passes. CJ Stroud led a pivotal drive in the third quarter, culminating in a touchdown pass to Xavier Johnson that gave the Buckeyes the lead. The Ohio State defense, spearheaded by Michael Hall Jr., maintained strong pressure, limiting Notre Dame's offensive possessions. Mayan Williams then secured the victory for Ohio State with a ground touchdown in the fourth quarter, solidifying an 11-point lead.
